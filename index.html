Our Mission

Empowering the Next
Generation of Investors

Founded in 2016 at UC Santa Cruz, the Slug Fund Investment Group exists to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real market practice. We believe every student, regardless of major, deserves access to financial literacy and investment knowledge.

We have weekly meetings where members discuss current market events and share insights. Our goal is to empower individuals with the knowledge, tools, and support they need to make informed investment decisions and achieve financial success.

We are more than a club, we are a community of curious minds committed to understanding the markets that shape our world.

Open to All Majors

You don't need to be an economics or business major. We welcome students from all disciplines who are eager to learn about investing and finance.

Industry Speakers

Our members have the opportunity to interact with professionals from leading financial institutions and companies.

Workshops

We host regular workshops on various topics in investing, finance, and career development.

What We Do

Five Pillars of
Financial Education

01

Market News and Analytics

Deep-dive into individual stocks across sectors. Learn valuation methods including DCF and comparable company analysis.

02

Financial Analysis

Understand how interest rates, inflation, geopolitics, and central bank policy shape global markets and investment opportunities.

03

Market Products

Learn about various financial instruments (options, futures, etc.) and how they are used in the market.

04

Career Development

Resume workshops and networking events with alumni and industry professionals.

05

Financial Literacy

General meetings for all students covering personal finance fundamentals: budgeting, credit, taxes, and building long-term wealth.

New Initiative

SFIG2 — The Student
Investment Fund

SFIG² is the next evolution of the Slug Fund Investment Group — a student-managed investment fund where members make real capital allocation decisions in live markets.

Going beyond theory and simulation, SFIG² puts students in the seat of a portfolio manager, conducting due diligence, debating investment theses, and executing trades with real accountability.

Real Capital. Real Decisions.

SFIG² members manage an actual fund, not a simulation. Every investment decision carries weight, building the discipline and rigor demanded by top financial institutions.

Goals & Objectives

Our mandate is long-term capital appreciation through fundamentals-driven equity selection. We target risk-adjusted returns while developing the next generation of investment professionals.

How to Get Involved

SFIG² membership is open to all current SFIG members.
